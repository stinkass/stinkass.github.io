What is the Stink Association?

The Stink Association is a global community made to protect and defend the world’s stinkers. Many Stinkers are threatened and beaten just for being who they are, and we at Stink Association hope to be able to prevent this harassment from happening by building a support group.

How can I help?

You can become a patron on Patreon or make a one time payment to our PayPal. You can do this easily through the Donate page on our site.

How can I join?

You can request a free membership through our e-mail join [at] stinkass.org or you can become a patron on our Patreon.
