About Us

The Stink Association is a global community made to protect and defend the world’s stinkers. Many stinkers are threatened and beaten just for being who they are, and we at Stink Association hope to be able to prevent this harassment from happening and to fund the treatment of stinkers who have been hurt.

How did the Stink Association come to be?

Our founder Mr. Stink was frustrated with the harassment targeting him and his family. On November 27th of 1996, Mr. Stink decided to start the Stink Association with donations from friends and family, and began seeking members. Ever since, Stink Association has made great strides for all the stinkers of the world.

How can I help?

The best way you can help is to donate. Any amount goes great lengths to help, so don’t worry that a donation is too small. See how to donate here.

Why make a website?

It’s hard to find members offline. We created this site to help our organization grow. We are always glad to reach new members, and we hope that with our website we will be able to do just that.

How can I join?

Unfortunately, it can take time to create a website. Right now we are not taking new members through our site, but don’t worry! We are working hard to allow people to join our association through this site, and we hope that we will be able to let you join as soon as possible. Until then, you can always email join [at] stinkass.org and we can try to let you join the slow way.
